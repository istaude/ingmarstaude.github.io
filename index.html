--- layout: home title: Hi, I'm Ingmar subtitle: I study biodiversity change in the Anthropocene. cover-img: "/assets/img/IMG_4220.jpg" --- Welcome to my website! I am working as a PhD Student with Henrique Pereira at the German Centre of Integrative Biodiversity Research (iDiv). I seek to understand how and why the abundance and distribution of species changes under anthropogenic global change. My goal is to generate actionable and policy-relevant knowledge to help fight the ongoing loss of Earth's biodiversity.
